# 🩺 Swasthya Saathi AI

**Your AI-powered health companion for early detection, instant support, and smarter healthcare access.**

Our Deployment:- https://swasthya-saathi-ai-india.netlify.app/ 

---

## 📌 Overview

**Swasthya Saathi AI** is an intelligent healthcare assistant designed to make healthcare **accessible, affordable, and available 24/7**. It leverages Artificial Intelligence to provide **early disease prediction, symptom analysis, mental health support, and telemedicine access**, especially for rural and underserved communities.

---

## 🚀 Features

- 🤖 AI Health Chatbot  
- 🧠 Early Disease Prediction  
- 📊 Personalized Health Recommendations  
- 📞 Telemedicine Integration  
- 💬 Mental Health Support (MANAS Helpline – 14416)  
- 🌐 User-Friendly Interface  

---

## 🏗️ Tech Stack

- Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript  
- Backend: Firebase  
- AI Integration: OpenAI API  
- Database: Firebase Firestore  

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Healthcare accessibility and early disease detection remain major challenges, particularly in rural and underserved areas. Many individuals ignore early symptoms due to lack of awareness, high costs, and limited access to medical professionals.

---

## 💡 Solution

Swasthya Saathi AI provides:
- Instant AI-based health assistance  
- Early detection of potential diseases  
- Mental health support integration  
- Remote consultation capabilities
