The Problem

Healthcare Gaps
That Can't Wait

Millions across rural and underserved India lack access to basic healthcare. Early symptoms go unnoticed, mental health is stigmatized, and doctors remain out of reach.

🏥
Limited Healthcare Access
Only 1 doctor per 11,000 people in rural areas — far below WHO's recommended 1:1,000 standard.
🧠
Mental Health Stigma
80% of individuals avoid mental health support due to stigma, high cost, and lack of accessible resources.
⚠️
Late Disease Detection
Many ignore early symptoms due to low awareness or cost concerns, leading to expensive late-stage treatments.

Core Features

Everything You Need
for Smarter Healthcare

From reactive care to proactive wellness — Swasthya Saathi covers the complete healthcare spectrum.

🔬
AI Disease Predictor
Input your symptoms and receive a list of possible conditions with severity levels and recommended next steps.
💬
Mental Health Chatbot
24/7 conversational AI providing emotional support, stress management tips, and integration with MANAS Helpline (14416).
📹
Remote Consultation
Chat or video-based telemedicine with doctor recommendations for users who cannot access hospitals easily.
📊
Health Dashboard
Track your symptoms history over time and generate basic health reports to share with medical professionals.
🏆
Gamification
Daily health check streaks, rewards for healthy habits, and a leaderboard to make wellness engaging and fun.
🔔
Smart Alerts & Emergency
Automated reminders for hydration, sleep, and checkups — plus an emergency alert button for critical situations.
System Flow

How Swasthya Saathi
Works For You

A simple six-step journey from symptom input to comprehensive care — powered by AI, built with empathy.

1
Log In
Create a secure profile and access your personal health dashboard with ease.
2
Input Symptoms
Describe what you're experiencing — the AI listens and understands natural language.
3
AI Analyzes
Our hybrid ML + rule-based model processes your data and identifies patterns in real-time.
4
Get Insights
Receive possible conditions, severity levels, precautions, and advice on when to see a doctor.
5
Mental Support
Access the mental health chatbot for emotional support, stress tips, or crisis helpline connection.
6
Consult Remotely
Connect with a real doctor via telemedicine — no travel, no waiting rooms, no barriers.
